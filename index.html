What's Tested

Understanding meaning, tone, and structure in context.

Key Insight: The SAT tests whether you can determine the most appropriate word based on the surrounding passage context.
‚ö†Ô∏è

What Makes This Tricky

Words may have multiple meanings; only one fits the passage.

Example: "The word 'fine' could mean good quality, thin, or a penalty - context determines the right meaning!"

Strategy Steps

1 Context Analysis - Read the sentence and understand the blank's role
2 Bridge Word - Look for signal words that show relationships
3 Word Cluster - Predict the type of word needed
4 Process of Elimination - Rule out obviously wrong choices
5 Verification - Test your choice by reading the complete sentence
